Code Book

For each record in the data set, it is provided:
- Average and Average Value of the Standard Deviation of the triaxial Acceleration of the accelerometer 
(total acceleration) and the estimated body acceleration.
- Mean and mean values of the standard deviation of the angular velocity of the triaxial gyroscope.


- A vector of 84 characteristics with mean and standard deviation of time domain and frequency.

- The activity label.
- An identifier of the subject who performed the experiment.


- identifier of the subject who performed the experiment.
Subject


- Activities carried out, which may be:
Activity

WALKING,
WALKING_UPSTAIRS,
WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS,
SEATED,
STANDING
LAYING
